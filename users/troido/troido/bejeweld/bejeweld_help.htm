In the game Bejeweled, the goal is to make rows of at least three the same colors.
The rows can be either horizontal or vertical.
To get such rows, you can switch two adjacent colors with each other by clicking them.
You can, however, only switch them if there will be a pair of at least three colors.
If you manage to make multiple pairs with just one switching, you will get bonus points.
The goal is to make as many combo's as possible in 42 moves.
